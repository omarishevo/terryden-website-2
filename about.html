About Terryden Agencies

Connecting Opportunities, Empowering Growth

Company Profile

Terryden Agencies (TDA) is a business solutions and agency services firm headquartered in Mombasa, Kenya, dealing in construction, solid waste management, research & consultancy, and general supplies — alongside its core recruitment, representation, procurement and consulting services.

Executive Summary

Terryden Agencies is a dynamic, client-focused agency and business solutions firm based in Mombasa, Kenya. Combining the expertise of its founders, the company specializes in bridging opportunities between businesses, talent, suppliers, and service providers. Whether through recruitment, business representation, procurement, consulting support, or general agency services, Terryden delivers reliable, efficient, and innovative solutions that drive growth and operational excellence for its partners.

Vision

To be the leading agency partner in East Africa, known for connecting opportunities, empowering businesses, and fostering sustainable growth through integrity and excellence.

Mission

To provide seamless agency, recruitment, representation, and business development services that help organizations and individuals achieve their goals efficiently while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and ethical conduct.

Core Values

Innovation & Efficiency

Reliability

Collaboration

Building strong, long-term partnerships.
